Serving Our Community's Health Needs

MedLab Inc. supports a broad spectrum of community health initiatives — from substance abuse recovery to criminal justice and respiratory illness management.

💙 Substance Abuse & Mental Health

For over 28 years, MedLab has served courts, treatment agencies, and behavioral health programs with accurate substance monitoring. Our testing supports recovery programs by providing reliable data that counselors and case managers can trust.

💊 Pain Management

We work closely with pain management physicians to provide comprehensive urine drug testing that supports responsible opioid prescribing. Our panels detect both prescribed medications and illicit substances to promote patient safety and regulatory compliance.

⚖️ Criminal Justice

From probation departments to DWI programs and drug courts, MedLab provides fast, defensible drug and alcohol testing with full chain-of-custody documentation. We help criminal justice programs operate efficiently and maintain program integrity.

🫁 Respiratory Illness Detection

Our advanced RT-PCR respiratory panels detect viral and bacterial pathogens — including SARS-CoV-2, Influenza A & B, RSV, and Streptococcus A — to enable fast, accurate diagnosis and appropriate treatment decisions.
