Toxicology Testing Services

Comprehensive substance screening and confirmation testing using the most advanced analytical technology available — delivering results that are accurate, fast, and legally defensible.

Presumptive Toxicology Screening

Our presumptive screening tests use immunoassay technology to rapidly identify the presence or absence of substances in a urine specimen. Results are typically available through our secure online portal by the next business day after receipt of the specimen.

Presumptive results serve as an efficient initial screen, allowing clinicians and case managers to quickly identify patients who may require further confirmatory testing.

Turnaround Time

Results typically available within 24 hours of specimen receipt via our secure online portal.

Definitive Toxicology Testing

Definitive testing using LC-MS (Liquid Chromatography–Mass Spectrometry) and GC-MS (Gas Chromatography–Mass Spectrometry) provides quantitative confirmation of substances identified in presumptive screening, as well as detecting additional compounds not covered by immunoassay.

Definitive results are quantitative, highly specific, and suitable for use in legal and regulatory proceedings. They represent the gold standard in toxicology confirmation.

Technology Used

LC-MS (Liquid Chromatography–Mass Spectrometry) and GC-MS (Gas Chromatography–Mass Spectrometry) — the gold standard in confirmatory drug testing.
