COVID-19, Flu & RSV Testing

A single PCR test for COVID-19, Influenza A & B, and Respiratory Syncytial Virus (RSV). Same or next-day results, available at multiple locations, and covered by most insurance plans.

Know What You Have — Test Today

COVID-19, influenza, and RSV share many of the same symptoms. Our single-swab combination PCR panel identifies all three simultaneously so your doctor can recommend the right treatment.

Common Symptoms to Watch For

🤧

Cough

Persistent dry or wet cough, sometimes severe

🌡️

Fever / Chills

Elevated temperature, sweating, and chills

😮‍💨

Shortness of Breath

Difficulty breathing or feeling winded easily

😩

Fatigue

Unusual tiredness and body aches

👃

Congestion / Runny Nose

Nasal congestion or discharge

🤒

Sore Throat

Pain or scratchiness in the throat

What We Test For

🧬 Combination RT-PCR Panel

Single swab — four respiratory targets

  • COVID-19 (SARS-CoV-2)
  • Influenza A (Flu A)
  • Influenza B (Flu B)
  • Respiratory Syncytial Virus (RSV)
  • Streptococcus A (Strep A) — optional add-on
Why PCR?
RT-PCR (Reverse Transcriptase Polymerase Chain Reaction) is the gold standard in respiratory pathogen detection — far more sensitive and specific than rapid antigen tests. You get accurate answers, not guesses.
