Certifications & Accreditations

MedLab, Inc. maintains the highest standards of quality, safety, and regulatory compliance. Our laboratory holds the following certifications and accreditations.

Our Credentials

Every certification we hold represents our commitment to accuracy, quality, and the trust of our patients, partners, and the communities we serve.

🏅

CLIA Certified

Certificate No. 33D0000000

Certified under the Clinical Laboratory Improvement Amendments (CLIA) for high-complexity testing. CLIA certification is required by federal law for all laboratories performing tests on human specimens.

✅

CAP Accredited

CAP Accreditation No. 0000000

Accredited by the College of American Pathologists (CAP) — the gold standard for laboratory quality assurance. CAP inspects our lab every two years against the most stringent standards in the industry.

🔒

HIPAA Compliant

Compliance Review — Annual

Fully compliant with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). All patient data is handled in accordance with federal privacy and security rules.

🏛️

New York State Licensed

NYS DOH License No. 0000

Licensed by the New York State Department of Health (NYSDOH) as a clinical laboratory, meeting all state requirements for personnel, testing procedures, and reporting.

⚗️

SAMHSA / DOT Certified

Federal Workplace Testing

Certified for federal workplace drug testing under SAMHSA (Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration) and DOT (Department of Transportation) guidelines.

🛡

ISO 15189 Quality Standards

Quality Management System

Our quality management system is aligned with ISO 15189 standards for medical laboratories, ensuring consistent, reliable, and internationally recognized laboratory practices.
